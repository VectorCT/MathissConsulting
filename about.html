About MATHISS Consulting

Heritage, professionalism, and innovation.

The name Mathiss originates from the family name Mathivhulula, preserving cultural identity while symbolizing a modern and visionary consulting approach.

Founder & Managing Director

Mbofholowo Ungani Mathiss

Mbofholowo Mathiss is a multidisciplinary professional with extensive experience in urban planning, architecture, engineering, and construction management. His vision is to establish MATHISS Consulting as a trusted, innovative, and socially impactful consulting brand while building a lasting family and professional legacy.

Qualifications

  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • Diploma in Architecture

Professional Registrations
