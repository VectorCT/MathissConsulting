About MATHISS Consulting

Creative Planning & Development Solutions

A dynamic, multidisciplinary consultancy operating within the built environment, providing integrated services across town planning, architectural design, engineering coordination, and construction support.

Who We Are

MATHISS Consulting was established with a vision to deliver professional, structured, and innovative development solutions to individuals, developers, and investors.

We are driven by a commitment to excellence, combining technical expertise, regulatory knowledge, and real-world construction understanding to unlock the full potential of every project.

We specialise in guiding projects from initial concept to final construction, ensuring that every development is compliant, cost-effective, and practically executable within the South African regulatory framework.

What We Do

We offer a complete, end-to-end development service:

Town Planning Services

Rezoning, consent use, subdivision, consolidation, and land use applications.

Architectural Design

Concept design, municipal submission drawings, and detailed construction documentation.

Engineering Coordination

Integration of structural and civil engineering designs with certified professionals.

BoQ & Costing

Accurate material quantification and cost estimation for budgeting and tendering.

Project Advisory

Professional guidance from feasibility stage through to project completion.

Our Approach

We follow a structured and strategic development process:

Feasibility → Land Use Approval → Design → Engineering → Costing → Construction

What This Ensures

  • Full compliance with municipal and statutory requirements
  • Clear project planning and reduced delays
  • Cost control and financial transparency
  • Efficient coordination between all disciplines
