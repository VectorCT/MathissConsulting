Quantity Surveying

Our quantity surveying services give clients cost certainty through precise estimating, disciplined contract control, and transparent reporting.

Detailed Delivery Scope

Quantity surveying is the financial backbone of any construction project in the South African built environment. It encompasses cost planning, bills of quantities preparation, procurement management, and rigorous financial control from inception through to final account close-out. At MATHISS Consulting, we apply a disciplined, transparent QS methodology that gives clients full visibility over project expenditure and ensures every rand is accounted for.

Why This Matters

Without accurate cost management, construction projects are vulnerable to budget overruns, contractual disputes, and cash-flow disruptions that can stall delivery entirely. Professional quantity surveying provides the financial certainty clients need to make informed decisions and keep projects on track.

Our Approach

We begin with detailed cost estimates and feasibility assessments, then produce comprehensive bills of quantities and tender documentation that support fair and competitive procurement. Throughout construction, our team manages valuations, variations, and payment certificates while maintaining real-time budget tracking to safeguard the client's financial position.

What's Included:

  • Pre-contract cost planning and estimates
  • Bills of quantities and tender documentation
  • Procurement and bid evaluation support
  • Valuations, variations, and payment certificates
  • Cashflow forecasting and budget tracking
  • Final account preparation and close-out
