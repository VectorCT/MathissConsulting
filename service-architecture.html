Architecture

We create architecture that is practical, climate-responsive, and identity-driven, delivering spaces that perform as beautifully as they look.

Detailed Delivery Scope

Architectural design is at the heart of shaping how people experience the built environment in South Africa. It goes beyond aesthetics — encompassing functional space planning, climate-responsive design, and the creation of buildings that reflect the identity and aspirations of their communities. At MATHISS Consulting, we combine creative vision with technical rigour to deliver architecture that is both striking and purposeful, rooted in the unique context of each project.

Why This Matters

Thoughtful architectural design directly impacts how spaces are used, how buildings perform over time, and how communities connect with their surroundings. Without a clear design philosophy, projects risk becoming generic, inefficient, or disconnected from the people they are meant to serve.

Our Approach

We start with a deep understanding of the client's brief, site conditions, and regulatory landscape, ensuring every design decision is informed and intentional. From concept sketches through to detailed documentation, our team collaborates closely with engineers, quantity surveyors, and contractors to deliver cohesive designs that translate seamlessly from paper to built form.

What's Included:

  • Concept design and space planning
  • Detailed drawings and specifications
  • 3D visuals and design presentations
  • Interior and facade coordination
  • Sustainable material recommendations
  • Authority submission support
